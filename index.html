Projects

Internet of Things fascinates me. There are huge investments in the IoT space by major software and hardware players. I believe it's a natural progression from the internet of content, internet of creation and now the IoT. Currently I am working on a software platform to manage various "things" in a centralized application. I have begun creating POC's using Arduino to transmit sensor data over wireless Radio Frequency (RF). RF is low cost, non chatty, low power consumption communication method which makes it a better choice than WIFI or Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE). I am planning to interface sensors to various development modules so that my software is portable and can be used regardless of the hardware.
